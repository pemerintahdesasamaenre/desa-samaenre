Proposed Codebase Refactor Design

Applying KISS, YAGNI, and DRY principles across the Admin module.

CURRENT (MESSY)
  • ❌ admin/module/[id]/edit & admin/module/edit/[id] (Mixed routes)
  • ❌ Client-side fetching with useEffect (Slow & verbose)
  • ❌ Schemas duplicated in actions/ and validations/
  • ❌ Manual auth checks repeated in every action
PROPOSED (CLEAN)
  • ✅ Unified route pattern: admin/module/edit/[id]
  • ✅ Server-side fetching (Fast, SEO-friendly, KISS)
  • ✅ Single Source of Truth for schemas in lib/validations
  • ✅ Consistent use of protectedAction helper

Rencana Aksi (Action Plan):

  1. Konsolidasi Skema: Pindahkan semua Zod schema ke lib/validations/index.ts.
  2. Standarisasi Actions: Gunakan protectedAction untuk semua mutasi data.
  3. Server Components: Refaktor halaman List di Admin untuk menggunakan server-side fetching.
  4. Pembersihan Route: Hapus folder route yang redundan.
